Welcome to Telescope Bangladesh!

Telescope Bangladesh is a model of Solar System, Night sky and Outer Space in real time, with accurate positions of objects and lots of interesting facts.
:)
We hope you will have as much fun exploring the universe with our app as do we while making it :)

It's Free thanks to you

Since its beginning in 2011, Telescope Bangladesh did always have a free version you could play with - and we intend to keep it that way!
If you like our model, you can support it in many ways:

by sharing Homepage with your friends :)

by liking our Facebook page

by subscribing to our Youtube channel

by subscribing to our Newsletter

by purchasing the Desktop version

by getting the Android app and iOS app

by sending us Your Ideas and Feedback

by writing a blog and recording a video blog with Solar
